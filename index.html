Section Two The Monsoon Pattern
Rainfall Overview

When the Monsoon Builds

Rain does not arrive evenly across the year. Each Northeast Monsoon season, Malaysia enters a period of sustained pressure with heavier rainfall and more frequent intense downpours. The late-2024 peak marks the strongest monthly surge in the five-year record, revealing how seasonal monsoon cycles shape the country's flood risk.
